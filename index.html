ABC Taxis is a well established Cork taxi company. We have been serving the people of Cork for over 25 years. Our aim is to offer you, the customer a first class service at an affordable price. We are aware that value for money is at the forefront of most people's minds at the moment, as well as quality service. With our experience and attention to detail we aim to provide all of this, as well as being able to remove the stress and hassle that travel can entail. We have a range of vehicles to suit all requirements, including 4 and 8 seater cars and people carriers, with wheelchair access. We are also happy to offer corporate travel & account work as well as all domestic and commercial taxi work.
